## Study Strategy

1. **Understand the Pattern**: Read the comprehensive notes
2. **Practice Examples**: Work through the provided examples
3. **Solve Problems**: Use LeetCode or similar platforms
4. **Review**:  Revisit weak areas regularly
5. **Internalize**: Recognize patterns in new problems

## Key Insight

> **Pattern > Problem**
> 
> Instead of memorizing solutions to thousands of problems, learn to recognize which pattern applies to a problem. Once you see the pattern, the solution becomes intuitive.
